About Sun Temple

The Sun Temple in Ranchi is a magnificent architectural marvel dedicated to the Sun God. Located on the Tata Road, about 39 km from the city, the temple is built in the shape of a huge chariot with 18 wheels and seven horses. This unique structure stands as a symbol of devotion and architectural brilliance, attracting devotees and tourists alike. The serene surroundings of the temple enhance its spiritual ambiance, making it an ideal place for meditation and worship.

Visitors to the Sun Temple are mesmerized by its intricate carvings and well-maintained premises. The temple complex also houses a sacred pond, which holds great religious significance. Many devotees believe that taking a dip in this holy water can purify the soul and bring blessings. The temple is especially crowded during the Chhath Puja, when thousands of devotees gather to offer prayers to the Sun God.
