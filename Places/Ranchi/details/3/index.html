Jonha Falls

About Jonha Falls

Jonha Falls, also known as Gautamdhara Falls, is one of the most enchanting waterfalls in Jharkhand. It is situated near Ranchi and is formed by the Ganga River as it cascades down rugged terrain, creating a mesmerizing view. The falls drop from a height of around 43 meters, making them a popular destination for tourists and nature lovers seeking tranquility and scenic beauty.

One of the unique aspects of Jonha Falls is the presence of a Buddhist shrine nearby, which adds to the spiritual and cultural significance of the place. The surrounding lush greenery and rocky landscapes create a serene environment, ideal for photography and peaceful retreats. A well-maintained stairway with approximately 722 steps leads down to the base of the falls, offering visitors a thrilling yet rewarding descent to witness the waterfall up close.

During the monsoon season, Jonha Falls is at its peak beauty, with an increased water flow that amplifies its grandeur. The area also serves as a great picnic spot where families and travelers can relax amidst nature. Whether you are an adventure enthusiast or simply looking for a refreshing getaway, Jonha Falls is a must-visit location that showcases the raw natural splendor of Jharkhand.
