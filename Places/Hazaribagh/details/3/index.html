About Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary

Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary is a paradise for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. Spanning over a vast area, the sanctuary is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including tigers, leopards, deer, and various bird species. The lush forests and grasslands provide a perfect habitat for these animals, making it one of the most significant wildlife reserves in Jharkhand.

Visitors can explore the sanctuary through jeep safaris, which offer a chance to witness the rich biodiversity of the region. The scenic beauty of the sanctuary, with its hills, rivers, and open meadows, makes it a perfect destination for adventure seekers and photographers. The watchtowers set up inside the sanctuary allow tourists to observe animals in their natural habitat without disturbing them.

Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary is an essential part of Jharkhand's ecological conservation efforts. Whether you're an avid wildlife enthusiast or simply someone looking to connect with nature, this sanctuary provides an unforgettable experience.
