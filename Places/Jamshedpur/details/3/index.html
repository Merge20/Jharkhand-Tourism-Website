About Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary

Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary, located near Jamshedpur, is a haven for nature and wildlife enthusiasts. Spread over an expansive area along the Dalma Hills, this sanctuary is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including elephants, leopards, barking deer, sloth bears, and many bird species. The sanctuaryâ€™s dense forests and rolling hills create a perfect escape for those looking to explore the wilderness.

One of the key attractions of Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary is its thriving elephant population, which migrates through the region. The sanctuary also offers breathtaking trekking trails, allowing visitors to enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. Adventure seekers and wildlife photographers often visit this site to capture the beauty of untouched nature and its inhabitants.

Apart from wildlife spotting, Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary is known for its peaceful ambiance, making it a great destination for meditation and relaxation. The sanctuary also has eco-tourism initiatives, promoting responsible tourism and conservation awareness. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast or simply looking for a tranquil retreat in nature, Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary is a must-visit destination in Jharkhand.
